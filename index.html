Privacy Policy for Status Saver

Effective Date: November 27, 2025

This Privacy Policy informs you regarding the policies of Status Saver (referred to as "the App" or "We") regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our Status Saver mobile application.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

We respect your privacy. Your statuses are not uploaded, transmitted, or shared with us or any third parties. This App is designed solely to save media content (images and videos) locally on your device. We do not require or collect any personally identifiable information from you.

1. Information Collection and Use (Zero Data Collection)

Personal Data

We do not collect, store, or transmit any Personal Data or Personally Identifiable Information (PII), such as your name, email address, phone number, location, or unique identifiers.

Status Content

  • What we do: The App accesses media (images and videos) that are temporarily stored by the WhatsApp application on your device in the .Statuses directory.
  • What we don't do: This content is processed, viewed, and saved only within your device's local storage. We do not transmit, upload, or share this status media content outside of your device.

2. Permissions and Data Access

Storage Access (Mandatory)

The App utilizes the Android Storage Access Framework (SAF) to gain permission from you to access the specific directory containing the status files (Android/media/com.whatsapp/WhatsApp/Media/.Statuses).

  • Purpose: This permission is strictly used to read the temporary status files and to write (save) the selected files to a general public media folder (like Pictures/Movies) on your device, giving you permanent access to the content.
  • No Broader Access: We do not scan, read, or upload files
